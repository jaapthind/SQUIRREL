Hi, I'm Jaap.

This started as a personal project. My dad was incredibly frustrated because squirrels kept raiding our backyard fruit trees right before the harvest was ready.

I wanted to solve the problem for him. Using my 3D printer and CAD software in my bedroom, I designed and prototyped this modular, wrap-around barrier.

After finding the perfect angle that squirrels simply couldn't grip, our fruit was finally safe. Now, I'm manufacturing these kits to help other families in Berkeley protect their hard work.

Installation Guide

Ready in 15 minutes. All hardware included.

0

Inventory Your Kit

6 Standard Plates, 2 Hook Plates, 16 Bolts/Nuts, 32 Washers, Metal Base Sheet, and Tape.

1

Wrap the Base

Wrap the metal sheet around the trunk at your desired height (at least 4ft up). Seal the bottom edge tightly against the bark with tape.

2

Set the Anchor Plates

Hang the 2 hook plates over the top of the metal sheet, positioned exactly 180 degrees apart.

3

Interlock & Torque

Attach standard plates to the hooks. Ensure the 'U' slots face each other. Bolt together, filling gaps until the ring is complete and rigid.
